About

I am an undergraduate student at the School of Computer Science, Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, advised by Prof. Jing Li.

I will be joining Hong Kong Polytechnic University as a PhD student in 2026.

My research interests lie in the field of Natural Language Processing, particularly focusing on Large Language Models, Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards (RLVR), Code Intelligence, and Reasoning Optimization.

I have published papers at top-tier conferences including ACL, ICML, and CCL.
